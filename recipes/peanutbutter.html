Honey Peanut Butter recipe

Description

This is my absolte favourite snack ever. It's delicious, healthy and makes for a great pre-workout. It's also great to have alongside your coffee especially when you dn't have time to/don't want to get a proper meal.

Ingredients

  • A handfull of peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Roast the peanuts in a frying pan on low heat until they get a light brown shade
  2. Blend them to get a smooth paste
  3. Add in the honey and the salt and stir well
  4. Store it in a glass jar to eat it anytime

That's it!

Glad you made it this far! You might as well try the recipe. Enjoy!
