Iced Americano recipe

Description

This is a simpe coffee drink for the hot days.

It's a simple recipe, easy to make by even a 3 year old, yet really delicious. Stick around and learn how to make it yourself

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Iced water
  • Some sweetener of your choice (I personally like bitter coffee so I don't add any)

Steps

  1. Pour iced water into a large glass
  2. Add in a packet f instant coffee
  3. stir well
  4. Add your sweetener of choice (Optional)

That's it!

Glad you made it this far! You might as well try the recipe. Enjoy!
